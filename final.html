Final Project

Night Hunter is a shooter style JavaScript game where the objective
is to eliminate enemies in the allotted amount of time.
The controls are standard where the user the mouse it point and click to eliminate enemies. This may seem simple but remember
you are hunting in the dark, so that means the enemies may not always be visible! The game will end either when the time allowed
for each round is exceeded or when the user defeats all of the enemies. Good Luck!
